Special Needs School

Sankalp's State-of-the-art Campus

Sankalp's State-Of-The-Art Campus Opens Its Doors In 2020 At Kollapancheri, Poonamallee

Situated in a serene atmosphere spanning 1.35 acres, with a built-up area of around 36,000 sq ft, this is an ideal institution for learning.

The school is designed to accommodate students with special educational needs. The various factors taken into consideration include:

Safety

Flexibility and Adaptability

Predictability

Avoiding Physical Clutter

In Order To Minimize Disturbances and Enhance Learning

Salient features of the Campus

 Set amidst nature in a serene atmosphere

Spacious and well lit classrooms with excellent ventilation

Exclusive fully equipped vocational rooms for various skills

Landscaped Sensory Gardens

The structure is built without any architectural barrier thus making it a safe and secure environment for persons with special needs

Toilets/Restrooms designed with Grab Bars and Handles

Occupational Therapy Room

Speech Therapy Room

Yoga Fitness Gym

Skating Zone

Well equipped, full-fledged kitchen

Sign Boards allowing students to navigate easily

Sandpits in children’s play area

Library

Auditorium with a seating capacity of 150-200 persons

A Store for products made by students

Eco-friendly Campus with

Solar Panel Installations
Rain Water Harvesting
Grey Water Treatment Plant
Up-cycled Sensory Tyre Park
Vegetable Garden

The School provides transport facility to the campus.
