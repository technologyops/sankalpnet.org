Learning Centre and Special Needs School

Procedure:

Admissions for the Learning Centre for persons with Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) , Specific Learning Disability(SLD) , Global Developmental Delays (GDD) is open throughout the Academic year. .

Steps to be followed

Fix an appointment by calling our office staff on 9551396995.

Once the appointment is fixed the parent accompanied by the child has to be brought to our Campus for an Intake Assessment along with the relevant documents.

After the Intake Assessment is completed, feedback is given to the parents.

A five-day classroom observation follows the feedback.

The child is then admitted into a suitable group based on the collective inputs of the teachers.

Special Needs School admits children in the following groups:

Primary EIC      Age Group: 3 – 6/7 yrs
Intermediate Junior   Age Group: 7 – 10 yrs
Intermediate Group   Age Group : 10 - 14 yrs
NIOS                                      Age Group: 14 - 18 yrs
Sahayika (Work skill Training Group)   Age Group 14- 18yrs
Samyukt ( Vocational Job training Group)   Age 18 yrs- 25yrs

Allied Therapies

Occupational Therapy

Speech Therapy

For Appointments, Admissions and further enquiries you can contact the office or the Principal through e-mail or telephone and they will guide you through the Assessment and Admission procedures.
