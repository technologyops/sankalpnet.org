FAQS

1.At what age can I enroll my child?

You can enroll your child from the age of 4.

2.Will the school provide transport facility?

Transport facility will be provided in certain localities. However we can connect you with a private transport facility.

3.Will you help my child move to an inclusive school after training in this school?

If you choose the junior and intermediate groups, it may be possible to seek re-admission in mainstream schools. However this will depend on the level of difficulty the child is experiencing and also the school to where he/she intends to join.

4.What are the school timings?

The school timings are between 9:30 am and 3 pm at the Learning Centre and between 9 am and 3.10 at the Open School.

5.What is the fee structure?

You can contact our admin office for more information on this.

6.When do admissions open?

At the Learning Centre for Autism Spectrum Disorders admissions are open throughout the academic year. At the Open School for Specific Learning Disabilities admissions are open between the months of January and April.

7.Do you have after school remedial programme?

Yes, at the Open School for SLD, we provide after school remedial help to children from mainstream schools.

8.Is private appearance recognized for higher studies?

Yes, children who appear in the Secondary Examination (Std X) from the NIOS Board may choose to return to mainstream schools for their Senior Secondary Examination (Std XII) or may opt for NIOS Senior Secondary Examination .
